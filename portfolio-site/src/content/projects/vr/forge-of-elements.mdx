## Overview

Forge of Elements is a hand-tracked VR game built in Unity that invites players to explore a magical world by combining elemental orbs like fire, water, air, and earth. Each discovery leads to new transformations in the world, from sprouting trees to crafting a crane.

<YouTube id="-XlfifLQECY" />

Explore the full project details on [FoVR Interactive](https://fovrinteractive.net/projects/forge-of-elements/) or play it on [Meta Quest Store](https://www.meta.com/experiences/7956388831140201/).

## Key Features:
- ✅ Element Combination System – Intuitive VR mechanic for fusing elements.
- ✅ World Progression – Unlock new items and areas by combining elements with world objects.
- ✅ Hand Tracking Support – Full support for gesture-based gameplay.
- ✅ Themed Environments – Starting with a Viking world and expanding into others.
- ✅ Hidden Object Reveal – Reveal and animate game objects when interacting with elements.

## Technology Stack:
- Engine: Unity
- Programming Language: C#
- VR SDK: Meta Quest (Oculus SDK)
- Art Assets: Synty POLYGON

## Project Goal & Learning Outcomes:
- 🎮 Deliver a progression-driven sandbox VR experience.
- 🧠 Explore intuitive design for natural hand-tracked interaction.
- 🌍 Design scalable world-building logic for future expansions.
